About the UEFA Champions League Trophy

The UEFA Champions League trophy is one of the most recognized and prestigious trophies in world football. It is awarded annually to the winning club of Europe’s premier competition. Its elegant design and iconic shape make it a true symbol of football excellence.

Trophy Details

The current version of the trophy was introduced in 1967, designed by Jörg Stadelmann. It stands 73.5 cm tall, weighs 7.5 kg, and is made of sterling silver. Clubs that win the competition five times, or three in a row, earn the right to keep the original trophy.
